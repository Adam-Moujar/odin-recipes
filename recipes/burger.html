Burger

Description:

Incredibly simple but amazingly delicious meat burger patty recipe!

Ingredients:

  • Large egg
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Ground beef
  • Dry bread crumbs

Recipe:

  1. Preheat outdoor grill and lightly oil grate
  2. Whisk egg, salt and pepper together in medium bowl
  3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs, mix well until blended
  4. Form into 4, 3/4 inch thick patties
  5. Place patties on preheated grill, cook 6-8 minutes per size
  6. Serve!
