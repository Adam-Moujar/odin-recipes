Shawarma

Description:

The recipe is for cooking delicious chicken shawarma with classic Yogurt sauce.

The serving can be done by wrapping it with pita bread along with salad or by serving it on a plate.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Spices: coriander, cumin, cardamom, paprika, cayenne, salt & pepper
  • Spice control: if you don't want it spicy

Sauce options for Shawarma:

Traditionally shawarma is served with Yogurt Sauce

They are quite easy to make, all they require are:

  • salt & pepper
  • yogurt
  • cumin
  • garlic
  • lemon

Recipe:

First marinate your chicken overnight, mix the choice marinade in a zip lock bag, put your chicken in the bag and leave it in the fridge overnight

Remove the chicken from the bag and add the yogurt sauce

Cook the chicken, on the stove or BBQ

Rest for 3 mins

Serve!
